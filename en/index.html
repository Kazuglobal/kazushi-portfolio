About

While working as an elementary school teacher in Mito City, Ibaraki Prefecture, I am expanding the possibilities of educational practice in a wide range of fields including AI education, sports analysis, and international exchange activities.

Profile

  • Kazushi Odate
  • Elementary School Teacher / AI Literacy Education Researcher
  • Graduated from University of Tsukuba, Faculty of Education
  • 8 years teaching experience (Mito City Elementary School, Ibaraki)
  • AI Education, EdTech, International Understanding Education

Educational Philosophy

I pursue education that nurtures the "creativity" and "coexistence skills" necessary for children to survive in a rapidly changing society. The development of technology, including AI, is significantly changing our lives and work. In such an era, the ability to proactively utilize technology and collaborate with people of diverse values is essential.

Vision

I aim to create a learning environment that maximizes the potential of each child through the integration of education and technology. I strive to explore new forms of education beyond conventional frameworks and pioneer the future of education through practice.
